Concept

Mortgage Save was born out of a desire to evaluate personal finances around UK property mortgages. The app provides quick unhindered calculation of mortgage loan repayments in instance of a potential house move or remortgage. It also gives insight into the effect of regular over payments to the loan, providing instant feedback as to the reduction of the mortgage term and saving against interest accrual at end of term.

Personal finance and planning was not a topic taught during the developerâ€™s schooling and providing this quick tool gives information to the user on the impact of mortgage interest and term against the initial loan can be altered.

Design

A simple design was laid out on paper and then formalised in Figma to prepare for user interaction. A UX/UI Designer was consulted to finesse and finalise the layout and screens. After some work-shopping with friends I realised that the terminology common to myself having worked close to the mortgage industry was not to be taken for granted. Additional screens were added with expanded information descriptions to the user experience.

Result share functionality was also added to allow users to store and send calculation output to others for consideration.

Challenges

XCTests were used in the development of the app to ensure accuracy of the results generated. Error handling is managed and presented to the user via an UIAlert to present the error occurrence and provide resolution instruction.

The future

Although the current version of the app provides users with a base level of information there are plans to expand the features on offer in the future to include the following:

  • Estimated total loan amount calculator
  • Integration of mortgage rate data from market API
  • Foreign currency support
