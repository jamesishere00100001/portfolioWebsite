Concept

Managing health care for a pet is challenging whilst maintaining a busy life of your own. Without keeping on top of regular booster vaccinations and worming etc a pet can become sick or worse. Brian looks to resolve these potential oversights by keeping all this information in one place, that is readily shareable with others who may take responsibility for their care.

Design

The UI design was designed to be simple and intuitive in its layout and presentation of information whilst allowing a second tier of detailed information to be held beyond. I worked with professional UX/UI Designer to establish the layout and flows. We have many future features for the product therefore we endeavoured with our initial design to allow for this expansion without creating a jarring experience to the user.

The tech design uses a Realm package to facilitate the CRUD local database functionality while allowing for future integration with a remote server interface for expansion and access to web and non-iOS platforms. The codebase is built using UIKit due in part to the previous experience of the developer and the breadth of the APIs available being beneficial to upcoming features. Currently the build employs PhotosUI, EventKit and StoreKit to support the existing feature list.

The future

The app is currently available across 175 countries and provides users with support in caring for their pets. We are in the process of preparing the roadmap for the next year with scheduling out the feature releases to further expand the functionality to our users.

Upcoming features include:

  • iCloud integration
  • Profiles (Owner, dog sitter, dog walker etc.)
  • Emergency contacts card
  • Vet, insurance and 'next of kin' share tiles
  • Weight tracking with graph
  • Medication tracking with notes
