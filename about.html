About me

Prior to pursuing a career in software engineering I trained and worked as Building Surveyor for a number of large firms in central London before establishing and operating my own private practice.

Working as a Surveyor I project managed commercial and residential projects from feasibility through design and construction. Inspected and effectively communicated complex defects and curated remedial proposals. Managed individual contracts and compliance whilst generating pipeline work.

After running my firm successfully for six years I felt the need to explore a new sector for which I was passionate. Transitioning into software engineering has thus far been a fantastic balance of daunting and challenging whilst becoming increasingly exciting and satisfying.
